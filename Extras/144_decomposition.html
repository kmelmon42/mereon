144 Polyhedron Decomposition

The disdyakis dodecahedron decomposes into three polyhedra
whose faces partition all 144 triangles with zero overlap.
144 = Cube ∩ Octahedron ∩ RD

The Three Polyhedra

Cube
6 {100} planes · d = 1/2
48 faces · 8 △ per □ face
Vertices: 8 at (±½, ±½, ±½)
Octahedron
8 {111} planes · d = 1/√3
48 faces · 6 △ per △ face
Vertices: 6 at (±1, 0, 0) etc.
Rhombic Dodecahedron
12 {110} planes · d = 1/√2
48 faces · 4 △ per ◇ face
Vertices: 14 (8 cube + 6 oct type)
